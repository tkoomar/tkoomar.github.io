About Me

I study the genetic intersection of language development and the cognitive functions underlying neuropsychiatric conditions. I rely on a variety of computational and statistical tools like machine learning and cloud infrastructure. I am also passionate about communicating science and the duty of scientists to thoughtfully share their research the rest of society.
